The San Francisco Walia Sports Club is a member of ESFNA.

Walia FC is member of the Ethiopian Sports Federation in North America (ESFNA). ESFNA was founded in 1983 to promote amateur soccer and other cultural events. Its goals include providing positive role models for the youth and promoting goodwill between the Ethiopian Community in North America and Americans. In addition to sports event, the federation also conducts cultural activities to provide opportunities for Americans to learn more about Ethiopia’s rich cultural history. The San Francisco Walia Sport Club will celebrate its 35th anniversary as a member of the ESFNA this summer. The sport club has won the annual ESFNA tournament in Los Angeles, CA (1987), Oakland, CA (1993) and Dallas, TX (1999). This year the 39th annual ESFNA event will be held in the City Silver Spring, Maryland, at the University of Maryland Capital One Field at Byrd Stadium from July 3 thru July 11, 2022. ESFNA soccer tournament has become a major week long gathering event. This year we expect to draw more than 50,000 in attendances ESFNA.
